Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Welcome to Nailify! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using Nailify, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Photos: When you use our nail analysis and try-on features, you provide photos of your nails and hands. These photos are processed by our AI systems and stored in our secure cloud database (Firebase Firestore).
  • User Account Information: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique identifier for you. No email, name, or personal details are required to use Nailify.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics, including app opens, feature usage, screen views, and interaction patterns to improve our services.
  • Device Information: We collect device type, operating system version, app version, and device identifiers for troubleshooting and analytics purposes.
  • Performance Data: Firebase Performance Monitoring collects data about app performance, load times, and network requests to help us optimize the app.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash reports and diagnostic information when the app experiences technical issues.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you enable notifications, we collect your device token to send you streak reminders and app updates.
  • Streak Data: We store your login streak information locally on your device using SwiftData.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Services: Process your photos through AI to analyze nail health and apply virtual nail designs.
  • Store Your Photos: Save your nail photos securely in Firebase Firestore so you can access your history.
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience.
  • Send Notifications: Deliver streak reminders and important app updates (only if you opt-in).
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues using crash reports and performance data.
  • App Security: Use Firebase App Check to verify legitimate app requests and prevent abuse.
  • Payment Processing: Process subscription payments through Superwall and Apple's In-App Purchase system (when applicable).

3. Third-Party Services

Nailify uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Secure storage of your photos and data
  • Firebase Analytics: App usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and diagnostics
  • Firebase Performance: App performance monitoring
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Firebase Remote Config: Dynamic app configuration
  • Firebase App Check: Security and abuse prevention

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3.2 OpenRouter

We use OpenRouter's AI models (GPT-4.1 Mini) to analyze nail health from your photos. Your photos are sent to OpenRouter's servers for processing but are not stored permanently by OpenRouter.

OpenRouter Privacy Policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

3.3 fal.ai

We use fal.ai's Seedream AI service to apply virtual nail designs to your photos. Your photos are processed through their API but are not retained after processing.

fal.ai Privacy Policy: https://fal.ai/privacy

3.4 Superwall

We use Superwall for subscription and payment management. Payment information is processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Superwall stores subscription status and user entitlements.

Superwall Privacy Policy: https://superwall.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Cloud Storage: Your photos and analysis results are stored in Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud infrastructure) with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Local Storage: Streak data and app preferences are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • We use Firebase App Check to verify that API requests come from legitimate instances of our app.
  • Anonymous authentication ensures no personal identifiers are required.
  • We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse and unauthorized access.

4.3 Data Retention

  • Your photos remain stored in Firestore until you delete them from the app or delete your account.
  • Analytics and crash data are retained according to Firebase's retention policies (typically 60 days for raw data).
  • You can delete individual photos or all your data at any time from within the app.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored photos within the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual photos or all your data from the app settings.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable analytics tracking in your device settings.
  • Notification Control: You can enable or disable push notifications in your device settings.
  • Data Portability: Contact us to request a copy of your data in a portable format.

6. Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information from our systems.
