‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the nail health analysis work?
Nailify uses advanced AI technology (GPT-4.1 Mini) to analyze photos of your nails. The AI examines factors like color, texture, shape, and overall condition to provide a health score (0-100) and personalized care recommendations. Simply take a clear photo of your nails in good lighting for the best results.
Why isn't my nail analysis accurate?
For best results: 1) Take photos in natural, bright lighting, 2) Ensure your nails are clean and visible, 3) Keep the camera steady and focused, 4) Avoid filters or heavy editing. Remember, this is an AI tool for guidance only‚Äîconsult a healthcare professional for medical concerns.
How do I use the virtual nail try-on feature?
1) Take or upload a photo of your hands with visible nails, 2) Choose a nail design from our library or upload your own custom design, 3) Wait a few seconds while our AI applies the design to your photo, 4) Save or share your result! Make sure your hands are well-lit and in focus.
Are my photos stored securely?
Yes! All photos are encrypted and stored securely in Firebase Firestore with industry-standard security. Your photos are private and only accessible by you. We never share your photos with third parties without your consent. You can delete your photos anytime from the app.
How do I delete my photos or account?
To delete individual photos: Go to All Photos ‚Üí Select X icon to Delete. To delete all data and your account: Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
What do I do if the app crashes or won't load?
Try these steps: 1) Force close the app and reopen it, 2) Restart your device, 3) Check for app updates in the App Store, 4) Ensure you have a stable internet connection, 5) Free up storage space on your device. If the problem persists, contact us at support@nailify.app with your device model and iOS version.
Why am I getting a "rate limit" error?
To ensure fair usage for all users, we limit the number of AI requests per hour/day. If you see this message, please wait a bit before trying again. Premium subscribers have higher limits. This helps us maintain service quality and control costs.
How do subscriptions work?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple's In-App Purchase system and automatically renew unless canceled 24 hours before the renewal date. You can manage subscriptions in Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions. All billing is handled by Apple, not Nailify.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are processed by Apple, not Nailify. To request a refund: 1) Go to reportaproblem.apple.com, 2) Sign in with your Apple ID, 3) Find your Nailify purchase, 4) Select "Request a Refund" and choose a reason. Refund policies are determined by Apple.
What's the daily streak feature?
Your streak tracks consecutive days you open the app and engage with your nail care routine. Opening the app each day maintains your streak. If you miss a day, your streak resets to 1. Enable notifications to receive daily reminders!
Can I upload custom nail designs?
Yes! When using the virtual try-on feature, you can upload your own nail design photos or inspiration images. Our AI will analyze the design and apply it to your hand photo. For best results, use clear images of nail designs with good lighting.
Why are notifications not working?
Check: 1) Settings ‚Üí Nailify ‚Üí Notifications is enabled, 2) Your device has "Do Not Disturb" turned off, 3) You haven't disabled notifications within the Nailify app. Go to Nailify Settings ‚Üí Notifications to re-enable streak reminders.

üîß Troubleshooting Guide

App Won't Open or Keeps Crashing

  • Force quit the app and reopen it
  • Restart your iPhone or iPad
  • Update to the latest version of Nailify from the App Store
  • Update your iOS to the latest version
  • Check if you have at least 500MB of free storage
  • Reinstall the app (note: this may delete local data)

AI Analysis Taking Too Long or Failing

  • Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi recommended)
  • Try taking a new photo with better lighting
  • Ensure the photo is under 10MB in size
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Wait a few minutes if you've hit the rate limit

Photo Upload Issues

  • Grant Nailify permission to access your Photos (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Photos)
  • Check if the photo is in a supported format (JPEG, PNG, HEIC)
  • Try taking a new photo with the in-app camera
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection

Camera Not Working

  • Grant Nailify camera permission (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Camera)
  • Check if another app is using the camera
  • Restart your device
  • Try using a photo from your library instead
